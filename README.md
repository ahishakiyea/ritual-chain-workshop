# ProgressiveAIBounty – Progressive Disclosure

This contract uses **progressive disclosure** where answers are revealed in stages over time, providing different levels of access to different stakeholders.

## How it works
1. **Commit phase**: Participants submit hashed answers
2. **Reveal phase**: Participants reveal their answers (basic)
3. **Partial reveal phase**: Participants share partial answers
4. **Full reveal phase**: Participants share complete answers
5. **Finalization**: Owner selects winner after full reveal

## Why progressive disclosure?
Provides controlled access to information, protecting participant privacy while ensuring transparency.
