# Reflection: Progressive Disclosure in Bounties

Progressive disclosure provides controlled access to information over time. This balances privacy needs with transparency requirements.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments are public. Answers are revealed in stages at different times. Access is logged and tracked.

**AI vs Human:** AI can judge submissions at each stage. The owner finalizes the winner after full reveal.

**Advantages:** Protects participant privacy, provides controlled transparency, enables multi-stage evaluation.

**Trade-off:** More complex workflow, but increased privacy and control.
